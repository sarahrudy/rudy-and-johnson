#### Summary
Welcome to Rudy & Johnson -- our take on the classic Rotten Tomatillos project for Mod 3 Front End students at Turing. In our app, you'll be able to see a variety of movies that have been reviewed. For each movie, you'll be able to view various details including a summary, a rating, and a trailer. Thank you for stopping by to check out our app! üçø

#### Instructions for Running and Viewing
1. Clone down this repo. 
2. Once you've cloned the repo to your machine, run npm start.
3. A browser window should open up with our film review site, 'Rudy & Johnson'. Here, you can view reviewed movies, search for specific movies as well as view trailers and details about the movies. Happy viewing!
